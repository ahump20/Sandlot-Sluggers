## ğŸ¯ Strategy Tips

### Batting Strategy
1. **Watch Pitcher's Speed**: Faster pitchers = less time to react
2. **Character Power Matters**: High power = more home runs
3. **Be Patient**: Wait for ball to get close before swinging
4. **Learn Timing**: Practice makes perfect!

### Character Selection Strategy
- **Need Home Runs?** Choose Slugger Sam or Home Run Hank
- **Want to Win Close Games?** Choose Speedy Stella for base stealing
- **Prefer Balanced Play?** Choose Captain Casey
- **Love Defense?** Choose Golden Glove Gina

### Stadium Selection Strategy
- **Learning the Game?** Start with Easy stadiums
- **Want a Challenge?** Try Hard stadiums for tougher AI
- **Need a Boost?** Sunny Shores wind helps power hitters
- **Testing Skills?** Forest Field shadows add difficulty

### Winning Tactics
1. **Maximize Early Innings**: Score runs early for comfort
2. **Use Power in Key Moments**: Save best swings for bases loaded
3. **Adapt to Stadium**: Use stadium features to your advantage
4. **Stay Focused**: Consistency beats lucky swings
